Green and kind to the plant

The global COVID-19 pandemic is changing how consumers view the world, and their place in it. That’s reflected in the latest trends in food and beverages that reveal the importance of:

  • Locally sourced, fewer food miles and seasonal
  • Reducing waste

But our unique research also shows your consumers won’t compromise on quality, experience and indulgence. Values we all share!

Conscious indulgence

Our insights reveals that high on the agenda are treats with a wellness boost, whether that’s plant-based, low or no sugar, or added enrichment. We have the recipes for all these demands and more. Creating feel-good moments to cherish is hugely important to your consumers right now. But it’s not just about pure indulgence. They want ‘ healthier indulgence’ . Join our webinar to learn how to cater to your consumers’ latest tastes.
